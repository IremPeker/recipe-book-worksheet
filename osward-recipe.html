Vegan Tacos

For these recipe you need:

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil.
  • 1/2 onion, sliced.
  • 2 teaspoons chopped seeded jalapeño chile.
  • 1 12-ounce package soy chorizo (sometimes labeled Soyrizo), casing removed.
  • 1 15.4-ounce to 16-ounce can vegetarian refried black beans.
  • 12 corn tortillas, warm.
  • Diced onion.
  • Chopped fresh cilantro.

Did you buy all the ingredients? Then follow these steps:

  1. Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onion and jalapeño; sauté until tender, about 10 minutes. Add soy chorizo and cook until beginning to brown in spots, stirring often, about 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook beans in heavy small saucepan over low heat until heated through, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stack 2 warm tortillas for each of 6 tacos. Spread scant 2 tablespoons beans over each stack. Top with soy chorizo mixture, dividing equally. Sprinkle with diced onion and cilantro.
